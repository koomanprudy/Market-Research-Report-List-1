<p><p>The handheld circular saw market is divided into two main types: cordless and wired. Cordless circular saws offer the advantage of portability and ease of use, allowing for greater mobility without the constraint of a power cord. They are powered by rechargeable batteries, making them ideal for remote or outdoor projects. In contrast, wired circular saws are powered through an electrical outlet, providing continuous power for extended cutting tasks and typically offering more robust performance for heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to varying user needs in construction and woodworking.</p></p>
