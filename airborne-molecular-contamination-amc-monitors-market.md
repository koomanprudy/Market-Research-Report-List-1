<p><p>The Airborne Molecular Contamination (AMC) monitors market comprises two primary types: multi-point sampling systems and single-point sampling systems. Multi-point sampling systems are designed to simultaneously monitor multiple locations within a facility, providing a comprehensive analysis of airborne contaminants across various zones. In contrast, single-point sampling systems focus on monitoring a specific area, offering detailed data about environmental conditions in a targeted location. Both systems are crucial for maintaining cleanroom environments and ensuring product quality in industries like semiconductors and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
