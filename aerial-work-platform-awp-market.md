<p><p>An Aerial Work Platform (AWP) is a mechanical device designed to provide temporary access for people or equipment to inaccessible areas, often used in construction, maintenance, and other industrial applications. Industries such as construction, maintenance, and warehousing are adopting AWPs to enhance productivity while minimizing risks associated with height work.
