<p><p>The gas sampling probe market is categorized into straight and curved gas sampling probes. Straight gas sampling probes are designed for direct and efficient sampling in linear systems, offering a straightforward path for gas collection. In contrast, curved gas sampling probes are engineered to navigate complex piping layouts, allowing for sampling in confined spaces while minimizing pressure loss. Both types are essential for accurate gas analysis in various industrial applications, including environmental monitoring and process control.</p></p>
