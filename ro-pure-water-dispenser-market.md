<p><strong>The RO Pure Water Dispenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU RO Water Dispenser</li><li>POE RO Water Dispenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO Pure Water Dispenser market includes two primary types: Point-of-Use (POU) and Point-of-Entry (POE) RO Water Dispensers. POU systems are installed at specific outlets, delivering purified water directly for drinking and cooking, ideal for residential and small commercial use. In contrast, POE systems treat water at the main supply source, ensuring that all water entering a building is purified, making it suitable for larger commercial or industrial applications. Both types cater to varying customer needs for convenience and safety.</p></p>
