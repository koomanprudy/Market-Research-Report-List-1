<p><p>The exoskeleton systems market is categorized into three main types: lower body exoskeletons, full body exoskeletons, and upper body exoskeletons. Full body exoskeletons support the entire torso and limbs, enhancing overall physical capabilities and reducing fatigue in industrial settings. Upper body exoskeletons focus on the arms and shoulders, providing support for repetitive tasks, improving ergonomics, and decreasing the risk of injuries.</p></p>
