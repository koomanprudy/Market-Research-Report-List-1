<p><p>Air-Source Heat Pump Systems (ASHP) utilize external air to provide heating and cooling for residential and commercial spaces. There are two main market types: Air-to-Air and Air-to-Water. Air-to-Air systems transfer heat directly to the indoor air, making them ideal for heating and cooling living spaces. In contrast, Air-to-Water systems heat water for radiators, underfloor heating, or domestic hot water, offering greater versatility in applications. Both systems contribute to energy efficiency and reduced carbon emissions.</p></p>
