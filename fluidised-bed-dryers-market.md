<p><p>Fluidised bed dryers are classified into two main market types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type fluidised bed dryers operate on a discrete batch of material, allowing for flexibility and precise control over the drying process, making them suitable for varying product sizes. In contrast, continuous type fluidised bed dryers maintain a constant flow of material, providing a consistent drying environment ideal for high-volume production. Both types cater to diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, enhancing efficiency in drying operations.</p></p>
